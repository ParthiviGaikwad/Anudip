White Sauce Pasta

Rich and creamy pasta with a classic white sauce.

Delicious White Sauce Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, corn, carrots, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil the pasta in salted water as per the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Vegetables: In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté the mixed vegetables until they are slightly tender. Set aside.
  3. Make the White Sauce: In a separate pan, melt butter on low heat. Add flour and stir continuously for 1-2 minutes until it turns light golden.
  4. Add Milk: Gradually add milk to the pan, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Continue stirring until the sauce thickens.
  5. Season the Sauce: Add black pepper, salt, and Italian seasoning to the sauce. If using, stir in the grated cheese until it melts.
  6. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta and sautéed vegetables to the sauce. Mix well to coat everything evenly.
  7. Serve: Garnish with a sprinkle of cheese or herbs and serve hot.
