Classic Veggie Sandwich

A quick and healthy sandwich recipe with fresh ingredients.

Classic Veggie Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat or multigrain bread
  • 2 tablespoons hummus or mayonnaise
  • 4-5 slices cucumber
  • 4-5 slices tomato
  • 4-5 slices bell pepper
  • Handful of spinach or lettuce leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Bread: Spread hummus or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Add Veggies: Layer cucumber, tomato, bell pepper, and spinach or lettuce on one slice of bread. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Assemble and Serve: Place the other slice of bread on top, press gently, and cut the sandwich in half. Serve immediately for the best taste.
